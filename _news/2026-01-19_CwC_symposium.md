Co-organized the 4th annual **Cells within Cells within Cambridge Symposium** at the Department of Pathology, University of Cambridge. The symposium brought together local researchers working on the broad theme of "cells within cells", featuring talks and discussions ranging from intracellular organelles and organisms to symbiotic relationships in superorganisms.
